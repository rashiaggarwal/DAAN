Things we Accept

We, are extremely happy to have your helping hand to support and making a difference in people's lives.Thank you for joining us in the survival, protection and development of the most vulnerable lives in India.Your contribution may bring smiles to someone's life. This is a one-stop destination for you if you want to give away anything. We have a large network of NGOs which work in a variety of fields. We connect you and these NGOs so that those who want to donate can easily donate to any cause they feel for.

Toys We channelize the non–utilized toys from near, affluent areas to underprivileged children. It connects the two extremes of the society i.e. less fortunate kids with the more fortunate kids.

Clothes Your clothing donations make a big difference to individuals who are struggling to make ends meet. Donating clothing can enable a child to prosper at school among her peers. Clothes donations can keep a family warm at night when the temperatures drop.

Study Materials Books (note books, text books, reference books) and stationary items (pen, pencil, scale etc) can help those students who have zeal to study, but unable to purchase books. The text books and reference books will be distributed directly to students who are financially weak and unable to purchase books.

Sanitary ‘No one should have to choose between sanitary items and a meal'. Charity calls on women to donate their old handbags filled with hygiene products to the homeless.

Gifts Surprise people, who need Love and affection, sending surprising gifts to spread smiles and happiness in their faces. Even a small gift can bring happiness and may fill their void.

Others If you wish to donate any other stuff, you can donate to bring change in people's lives and together we can step ahead to this chain of bringing change to the world.
